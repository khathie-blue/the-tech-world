Introduction to Cybersecurity

Understand the fundamentals of cybersecurity, including encryption, network security, and ethical hacking. This course covers the basic principles and tools used by cybersecurity professionals to protect systems and networks from cyber threats.

Course Topics:

  • Cybersecurity Principles
  • Encryption and Decryption
  • Network Security (Firewalls, VPNs)
  • Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing
  • Incident Response and Management

Prerequisites:

This course is suitable for beginners, but familiarity with basic computer networks can be helpful.
